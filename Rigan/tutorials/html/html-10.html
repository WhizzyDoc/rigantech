HTML Centered

To center HTML contents and/or elements, we just need to enclose them inside a <center> element.

Example:

<center>
  <h1>I an a centered heading</h1>
  <p>I am a centered paragraph</p>
  <img src="../images/b9.jpeg" />
</center>

Output:

I am a centered heading

I am a centered paragraph


As you can see the heading, paragraph and the image are all centered.

Warning! The <center> element is deprecated, it means that browsers may not support it anymore in the future.

Alternative

To center texts and images, we can use CSS styling.

The style="text-align: center" attribute can be used as an alternative to the <center> element.

Example:

<div style="text-align: center">
  <h1>I an a centered heading</h1>
  <p>I am a centered paragraph</p>
  <img src="../images/b9.jpeg" />
</div>

Output:

I am a centered heading

I am a centered paragraph
