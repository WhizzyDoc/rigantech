HTML Text Formatting

Formatting text is very essential for web sites.

One of its uses is to help readers know the important parts of a wb content easily.

Elements for Formatting Text

All of the elements listed below are inline elements.

  • <b>: bolds at text
  • <i>: italicizes a text
  • <u>: underlines a text
  • <code>: defines a text as a code
  • <tt>: typewriter text
  • <small>: makes a text smaller
  • <big>: makes a text bigger
  • <em>: emphasizes a text, may be italicized, depending on the browser
  • <strong>: emphasizes a text, may be boldened depending on the browser
  • <mark>: marks a text like a highlighting pen
  • <q>: enquotes a text
  • <s>: strikes through a text
  • <sup>: converts text to superscript
  • <sub>: converts text to subscript

Example:

<b>I am a boldened text</b> <br />
<i>I am an italicized text</i> <br />
<u>I am an underlined text</u> <br />
<code>I am a code text</code> <br />
<tt>I am a typewriter text</tt> <br />
<small>I am a smaller text</small> <br />
<big>I am a bigger text</big> <br />
<em>I am an emphasized text</em> <br />
<strong>I am a strong text</strong> <br />
<mark>I am a marked text</mark> <br />
<q>I am an enquoted text</q> <br />
<s>I am a striked text</s> <br />
I am a <sup>superscript</sup> text <br />
I am a <sub>subscript</sub> text <br />
<b><i><u><mark><q>I am a mixed formatted text</q></mark></u></i></b>

Output:

I am a boldened text
I am an italicized text
I am an underlined text
I am a code text
I am a typewriter text
I am a smaller text
I am a bigger text
I am an emphasized text
I am a strong text
I am a marked text
I am an enquoted text
I am a striked text
I am a superscript text
I am a subscript text
I am a mixed formatted text
